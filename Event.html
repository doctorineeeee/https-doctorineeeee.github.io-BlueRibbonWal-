About the Blue Ribbon Walk Striving to foster a community of empathy and understanding. Inspirational guest speakers and advocates shed light on the impact of child abuse, equipping attendees with knowledge and resources to become champions of change. With your participation, we can make a lasting impact and ensure that no child is left to face the scars of abuse alone.
