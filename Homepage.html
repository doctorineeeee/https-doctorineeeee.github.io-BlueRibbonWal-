1 in 7 children have experienced child abuse

A heartbreaking statistic that demands our attention. Blue Ribbon SG, our annual charity event, aims to make a lasting impact on the lives of these vulnerable children.
Taking place during a dedicated month for child abuse awareness, our campaign seeks to increase awareness, raise funds, and advocate for the early detection and support of abused children in Singapore.
During the Blue Ribbon SG event, we encourage everyone to proudly wear the blue ribbon, an iconic symbol representing our commitment to protecting and nurturing abused children.
These specially designed blue ribbon pins will be made available across the island, serving as a visible reminder of our collective efforts to bring hope and healing to these young survivors.

Through this significant campaign

We strive to create a safe and supportive environment for abused children. Our goal is to empower individuals and communities to recognize the signs of abuse, provide assistance, and actively contribute to the recovery process.
By participating in Blue Ribbon SG, you become an advocate for change, standing up against child abuse and working towards a brighter future for every child in Singapore. Join us in this noble cause as we come together, wearing the blue ribbon with pride.
Your involvement in Blue Ribbon SG will help us raise vital funds, provide support to established organizations, and create a strong network of care for abused children.
Together, let us bring light to their lives, ensuring that no child in Singapore has to endure abuse in silence.
